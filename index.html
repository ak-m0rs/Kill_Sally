🧩 Type A — Hold Builder

Bottom holds, slow push‑ups, plank. Dynamic intensity based on your level.
Each session: add +5s to holds or +1-2 reps. Small gains compound into big endurance jumps.

⚡ Type B — Fatigue Resistance

Ladders, sub‑maximal sets. Trains your body to keep firing through accumulated burn.
Never train to failure here. Stop 2-3 reps before collapse to preserve recovery.

🎮 Type C — Simulation

Partial run (60-85% of song depending on phase). Controlled stop before failure.
This is NOT a full attempt. Stopping early on purpose keeps confidence high.

🌿 Type D — Recovery

Easy reps, short holds. Keep the adaptation signal without adding fatigue.

🏆 Attempt Days (5, 10, 14)

Full song attempts. Targets adjust dynamically to your baseline.
Technique during attempt:
— Stay tight in the bottom, never fully relax
— Slight forward lean engages chest more
— Breathe slow and steady, never hold breath
— Micro-adjust position during long holds
— Exhale on the way up, inhale on the way down
